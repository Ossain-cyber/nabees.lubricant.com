Certifications & Testing

Nabeess maintains a quality management system and uses accredited laboratories for viscosity, volatility, wear and oxidation testing. Our certifications include:

  • ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management
  • ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management
  • API approvals & sequence testing
  • OEM approvals and field validations

Representative Test Reports

Independent lab test reports are available on request. Our quality team compiles batch-level reports for customers and aftermarket distributors.

Oxidation Stability

Bench testing to measure oxidation and deposit formation under accelerated thermal aging.

Wear Protection

Pin-on-disk and engine wear testing to assess anti-wear additive effectiveness.
