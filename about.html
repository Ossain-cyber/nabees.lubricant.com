About Nabeess Lubricants

Nabeess is a technology-driven lubricant manufacturer with a focus on performance, reliability and responsible manufacturing. Founded by engineers and chemists, our R&D team works closely with partners to tailor products for regional climates and application requirements.

We operate modern blending facilities, maintain rigorous quality control, and invest in laboratory testing programs. Our mission is to help customers reduce total cost of ownership through product longevity, fuel economy gains and lower maintenance overhead.
